About

Shannon was first introduced to community—based work as an AmeriCorps VISTA working for a community development corporation in Memphis, TN, just south of downtown. That experience grew through subsequent positions, most notably as a program officer at the Community Foundation of Greater Memphis and as a project manager for the UrbanArt Commission, a non-profit that facilitates public art throughout Memphis neighborhoods. Along the way she also worked with arts organizations and voluntary disaster relief agencies. Shannon holds a Masters degree in City and Regional Planning and lives in Memphis, TN with her husband and son.
